THE BENEFITS

-Environmental conservation protects biodiversity

Environmental cnservation preserves ecosysytems,protecting countless species of plants,animals,and microorganisms.This maintains the delicate balance of nature,ensures ecosystem services like pollination and pest control, and supports species evolution

-Ensures clean air and water

Conservation efforts reduce pollution, protecting air quality and water sources. clean air and water are essential for human health, wildlife survival and ecosystem balance.This also supports agrivulture, industry and recreation.

Mitigates Climate Change

Conservation plays a key role in regulating the global climate. Forests absorb carbon dioxide' wetlands manage water cycles, and oceans absorb heat and carbon.This helps to reduce the extreme weather events and sea-level rise
